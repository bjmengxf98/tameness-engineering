# Social posts for Tameness Engineering

## English short post

I propose a new concept: **Tameness Engineering**.

Prompt engineering gives the model commands.  
Context engineering gives the model an environment.  
Harness engineering gives the model tools.

But AI agents also need habits.

The hard part of building reliable agents is not writing one perfect prompt. It is shaping behavior over time: observing failures, identifying drift patterns, correcting gently, reinforcing good behavior, and stabilizing the agent in real environments.

AI agents are not written.  
They are tamed.

## 中文短帖

我提出一个新概念：**Tameness Engineering，驯服工程**。

提示词工程给模型命令。  
上下文工程给模型环境。  
Harness 工程给模型工具。  
对齐工程给模型边界。  
而驯服工程，给模型习性。

智能体不是一次性写出来的，而是在真实环境中通过观察、识别、校正、强化和稳定，慢慢驯出来的。

智能体不是写出来的。  
智能体是驯出来的。
